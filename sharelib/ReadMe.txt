Sharelib is the base library.

ShareOS is composed of "elements" created, managed and accessed by "authors" and version controlled
through defining branches. An author can be a group or single person.

Each element starts at its base with a "Definition". An author within a particular group defines this
type, function, parameter, variable, namespace, component, or projects, etc.


A type, function, variable can belong to one or more namespaces.

By visibility: public, protected, private.
